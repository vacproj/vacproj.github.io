what we do

Each year, the Lincoln College Vacation Project runs two or three free week-long holidays for children living in the Oxford area who are involved with social services, in order to give them a break from whatever difficulties they may be experiencing and an opportunity to try new things.

50th anniversary celebrations 🎉

In 2021, we will be celebrating 50 years of VacProj!
To commemorate this, we will be organising a number of festivities in and around Lincoln College. To reserve your place at our 50th anniversary dinner, please contact Aisling at vacation.project@lincoln.ox.ac.uk.

get in touch!

If you are a parent or a social worker looking to nominate a child for VacProj, please contact our Social Services Liaison Officers at vacproj@ox.ac.uk. They will be able to advise you on how to apply for this year’s holidays and provide more information about the activities we run.

vacproj@ox.ac.uk

If you have any more general queries about our aims, or if you are interested in getting involved with VacProj, please contact Aisling, the charity’s President, at vacation.project@lincoln.ox.ac.uk.
